Our Services

Finance solutions for every situation

From declined applications to urgent bridging needs, we have access to a wide panel of specialist lenders.

Declined Mortgage Solutions

Turned down by your bank or broker? We work with specialist lenders who consider complex cases — adverse credit, self-employed income, non-standard properties and more.

Bridging Loans

Need fast, short-term funding? Our bridging finance solutions can be arranged in as little as 48 hours — ideal for auction purchases, chain breaks and refurbishment projects.

Development Finance

Funding for ground-up developments and major refurbishments. We source competitive rates from specialist development lenders with flexible drawdown schedules.

Second Charge Loans

Release equity without remortgaging. Second charge lending lets you keep your existing rate while accessing additional funds secured against your property.

Commercial Mortgages

Finance for offices, retail units, HMOs and mixed-use properties. We match your commercial lending needs with the right lender from our specialist panel.

Portfolio Lending

Multi-property investors need specialist lenders who understand portfolio structures. We arrange finance across buy-to-let portfolios of all sizes.
