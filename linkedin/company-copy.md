## About Section *(2000 chars max)*

itsadecline.com is a fully automated pipeline for equity release and second charge mortgage cases in the UK.

Traditional mortgage broking is slow, manual, and full of friction — chasing clients, repeating data entry, waiting on lender callbacks. We've removed all of that.

**How it works:**
A client pays a £295 commitment fee to open a case. This upfront payment filters out tyre-kickers and ensures every case that enters the pipeline is serious. From there, identity is verified digitally, an HM Land Registry (HMLR) title check is run automatically, loan-to-value is calculated against current property data, and the case is matched to relevant lenders. Offers are presented to the broker — no phone calls, no chasing, no manual paperwork.

**Built for property professionals:**
itsadecline.com is designed for mortgage brokers, IFAs, financial advisers, and property professionals who handle equity release or second charge mortgage enquiries. Whether you're placing one case a month or running a high-volume operation, the platform removes the admin overhead so you can focus on advising clients.

**What makes us different:**
- Commitment fee filters serious applicants before any work begins
- HMLR checks happen upfront — no title surprises later
- Fully automated from submission to offer presentation
- No calls, no email chains, no manual case management
- Transparent, fixed-cost entry for clients

The result: faster decisions, lower cost per case, and a cleaner experience for everyone involved.
